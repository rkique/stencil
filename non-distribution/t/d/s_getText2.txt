CONNECT UNRELATED WORDS VIA NEIGHBORS

Each day, there are 5 word pairs with a start and target word.


CLICK A WORD TO SEE ITS NEIGHBORS

Neighbors are words that frequently appear in similar contexts and convey
related meanings.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Example

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

whisper ⟶ shouting ⟶ scuffle

whisper and shouting are both exclamations.
A shouting match might lead to a scuffle.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Choose neighbors which relate to the target word. The progress bar shows the
remaining distance.
